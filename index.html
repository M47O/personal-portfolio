New devs

If you're a new programmer and need some direction or if you've never even touched a line of code but would like to start learning, please send me an email or drop me a DM on Twitter. I'd love nothing more than to help you start your journey. Learning software development has been the most rewarding decision I've made to date, and I'd hate to see anyone who's interested in it not start because they didn't know where to begin or because they let self-doubt get in the way. With the right tools and support, anyone can learn anything they set their mind to.
